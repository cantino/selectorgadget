SelectorGadget Bookmarklet

SelectorGadget is a tool to help you generate efficient CSS selectors. Just drag the bookmarklet to your bookmark bar, then go to any page and press it. You can then mouse over elements on the page to select them (green) and the tool will come up with a minimal CSS selector based on your choices. It shows everything matched by that selector in yellow. If you don't want any of that matches, click on them to reject them. The selector will be updated to reflect your changes. You can find the current version on GitHub, and please feel free to leave comments below.
Created by Andrew Cantino and Kyle Maxwell.
